Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 24, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how the Autoclave App ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. The app is designed to connect and control an autoclave controller, facilitating communication and adjustments via an MQTT server.

Information We Collect

  • Device ID: When you install the app, you are required to enter the Device ID provided by us.
  • Wi-Fi Network Details: From the app settings, you can view and modify the name and password of the Wi-Fi network to which the controller connects.
  • Operational Data: The app periodically queries the autoclave controller for data, such as current temperature, time remaining, and status.

How We Use Information

  • To facilitate communication between the app and the autoclave controller via the MQTT server.
  • To allow users to adjust autoclaving parameters such as temperature and time.
  • To display real-time operational data from the controller within the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. However, the following exceptions may apply:

  • Data exchanged between the app and the autoclave controller via the MQTT server.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, we may disclose information to authorities.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your information. Data exchanged via the MQTT server is handled through the HiveMQ MQTT broker, which is reliable and ensures encrypted communication for secure data transfer.
